Unlock Bootloader of Redmi Note 7/7s

To unlock bootloader of Redmi Note 7/7s follow the steps below. It is not only about Redmi Note 7/7s. Rather, the steps are more or less the same for all Xiaomi Devices which uses MIUI.

Before that note this: ALL THE DATA IN YOUR PHONE WILL BE ERASED

Step 1: Adding your MI account and enabling developer option.

  • You can only use your mobile data to unlock the bootloader. So, switch off the wifi and use your mobile data.
  • Add your Mi account on your phone if already done. (IGNORE)
  • Now enable developer mode.
  • In developer option, goto Mi Unlock status and follow the steps it is asking for.

Step 2: Downloading and installing the MI Unlock Tool

  • To download MI Unlock Tool, you can visit my website and download the MI Bootloader Unlock Tool.
  • Then extract the files in a folder where you can find miflash_unlock.exe
  • Open miflash_unlock.exe and click agree on some dialogue box.
  • On the top right corner, you can find a QR code click that and scan with your mobile phone and click sign in and ok.
  • Now you have to connect your mobile to Laptop/Desktop with a USB cable.
  • Now go to Fastboot mode by pressing the (volume down + Power) buttons together unless you can see the Fastboot screen.

Step 3: Final step, unlocking the bootloader.

  • Now, on your Laptop/Desktop you can see unlock, press unlock.
  • There will be two warning messages popped with Unlock anyway button press that.
  • Reminder: ALL YOUR DATA WILL BE LOST.
  • After this, your phone will power on automatically and now your phone is unlocked.
